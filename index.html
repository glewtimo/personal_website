About Me

I am a student in Oregon State's Online Post Baccalaureate Computer Science program. Before beginning my CS degree, I graduated with a Bachelors and Masters in accounting from Michigan State University. After graduation I spent a year working as a Financial Statement Auditor, during which time I realized my passion for technology and made the decision to pursue a degree in CS and change careers. I have a strong interest in software development, and I believe that my business education and work experience provide me with a unique perspective that I can leverage as I enter the field.

I also have many passions outside of the tech world. I am an avid runner and in October of 2019 I completed my first marathon. I also enjoy reading, playing video games, and hanging out with friends and family.
